Hello! My name is Sarah and I am a Graphic Designer living in New York City. Over the past six years I have worked in the book publishing industry, first in book covers and then in marketing, and I also do freelance work for other clients. My love of books comes across in my design work; I want every piece I create to tell a story, and I like to collaborate with clients to display their own narratives in a visual way. My aim is to deliver a clear message through clean, interesting design at the highest quality. I am available for work on a freelance basis. Please reach out to me to find out more!
